Daniel Miin

Co-founder, Operis Health AI & Finance Executive

Dan is the Co-Founder of Operis Health AI, a computer vision platform designed to automate documentation and improve medication safety in anesthesia. With over 15 years of experience as a finance executive, he has advised numerous healthcare companies and navigated more than $15 billion in M&A and capital markets transactions. His career spans leadership roles at prestigious firms including Jefferies, Brookfield, and Advanced Micro Devices as well as serving as Chief of Staff at Elekta.

Driven by a passion for building the next generation of healthcare solutions, Daniel actively mentors health tech and healthcare software startup founders. He currently lends his strategic expertise as a board member and advisor to several organizations, such as CareShield AI, morriganAI, and the Providence Hospice Foundation. His professional focus remains centered on leveraging technology, AI, and financial leadership to drive meaningful digital transformation within the health tech landscape.

Beyond his professional endeavors, Daniel maintains a keen interest in the evolving landscape of AI and the integration of smart home technologies. This curiosity about how intelligent systems can enhance daily life mirrors his work in healthcare, where he applies advanced machine learning to solve complex operational challenges. Whether exploring new automation tools or advising on AI identity, he is dedicated to the responsible and impactful deployment of emerging tech.
